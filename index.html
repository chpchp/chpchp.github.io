About

I recently graduated with a Master of Science from Tsinghua University studying distributed systems and storage systems under Professor Jiwu Shu and Youyou Lu as part of the Storage Research Group. My thesis focused on SmartNIC-based shared log system. During grad school, I had the amazing opportunity to collaborate with Qing Wang, Youmin Chen, Zhe Yang, Bohong Zhu, Junru Li, Jian Gao.

As an undergraduate at Central South University, I researched under Professor Weiping Wang .
